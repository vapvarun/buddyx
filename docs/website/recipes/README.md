# Recipes

Step-by-step walkthroughs for common BuddyX outcomes. Each recipe targets a specific result, not a single setting.

| Recipe | What you'll build | Time |
|---|---|---|
| [Match the demo design](./match-the-demo.md) | Your site looks like the published BuddyX demo on wordpress.org | ~30 min |
| [Customize your colors and fonts](./customize-brand.md) | Your site reflects your brand colors + typography | ~45 min |

---

## When to use a recipe vs. browse settings

- **Use a recipe** when you want a specific *outcome* (a branded site, the demo look, etc.). The recipe walks across multiple settings + plugins in the right order.
- **Browse settings** when you know the specific setting name (e.g., "site_loader_speed") and just want its reference. See the [Topic map](../../README.md#browse-by-topic-advanced).
